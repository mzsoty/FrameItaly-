**Sagre, dialetti, architettura rurale, vita reale.**

---

## What is FrameItaly?

FrameItaly is a curated stock photography platform where Italian photographers can upload and license their work — focusing on the Italy that travel guides forget.

- 🏘️ Rural architecture & small towns
- 🎉 Local festivals (sagre) & traditions
- 🗣️ Regional dialects & cultural identity
- 🧑‍🌾 Real everyday Italian life

---

## For Photographers

- Upload photos with advanced tagging (region, dialect, category)
- Fixed pricing tiers based on file size and license type
- **60%+ royalty** on every sale
- Reach international editorial buyers

## For Buyers (Publishers & Editors)

- Browse by region, theme, or cultural context
- Editorial, Commercial, and Extended licenses available
- Authentic imagery that stands out from generic stock
